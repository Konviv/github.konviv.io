More Services

Konviv is a mobile fintech startup creating a global digital financial identity and global credit score while implementing a new way to budget, track, manage finances, grow wealth, and access capital. Konviv users are able to receive incremental microloans through our peer-to-peer marketplace based on their Konviv score and get help paying it back through artificial-intelligence-driven notifications and budgeting. As users engage with the platform, Konviv uses machine learning to provide customized financial guidance, in turn their Konviv score, and access to additional capital, also increases.
